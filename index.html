About Us

WM Group was established on 1998 and we run a diversified business through our subsidiaries to meet a specific set of customer needs.

With over 20 office locations across Hong Kong and China, we have a strong team of over 600 staff, working diligently to bring out the best to our clients in the industry.

Since its inception, we have grown into a group of multi¬modal transportation companies, covering Air, Sea, and Land freight modes with services ranging from common feeder, international freight forwarding, logistics and supply chain, water front and freight terminal, container and cargo trucking to liner agency.
